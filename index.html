What is Carbon Footprint? A Carbon Footprint is the total greenhouse gas (GHG) emissions caused by an individual, event, organization, service, place, car, plane, train, or product, expressed as carbon dioxide equivalent (CO2e). The Carbon Footprint causes global warming.
